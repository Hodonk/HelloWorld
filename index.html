"The Journey of Elaina" (also known as "Majo no Tabitabi" in Japanese) is a fantasy adventure anime series based on a light novel written by Jougi Shiraishi and illustrated by Azuru. The story follows the adventures of Elaina, a young witch who travels the world, exploring different places and meeting various people.

Key Points:

  • Plot: Elaina is inspired by a book she read as a child, which recounts the travels of a famous witch named Nike. After becoming a witch herself, Elaina sets out on her own journey, encountering a variety of unique and sometimes challenging experiences along the way.
  • Characters:
    • Elaina: The protagonist, a talented and curious witch with silver hair and blue eyes. She travels alone, documenting her adventures.
    • Fran: Elaina's mentor who plays a significant role in her development as a witch.
    • Saya: A novice witch who becomes friends with Elaina and has a strong admiration for her.
  • Themes: The series explores themes of self-discovery, the importance of experiences, and the diversity of human nature. Each episode often presents a standalone story with its own moral or lesson.
  • Light Novels and Manga: The anime is based on the light novel series which has also been adapted into a manga. The light novels are well-received for their whimsical storytelling and rich world-building.
  • Anime Adaptation: The anime was produced by C2C and aired in the fall of 2020. It received praise for its beautiful animation, engaging storytelling, and the charm of its main character, Elaina.
